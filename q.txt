---
A ⟨compilation⟩ is a string of self-contained ⟨program segment⟩s; each segment
is either a ⟨main program⟩ or an ⟨external procedure⟩. All the segments of
a ⟨compilation⟩ will be associated together by the loader, but it is not 
necessary that all segments needed to complete a load be compiled together. 
A load must contain exactly one ⟨main program⟩.
